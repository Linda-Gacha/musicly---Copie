About Young Tunisian Artists

The Event Hub project creates a centralized platform for associations and clubs to showcase events, boost community engagement, and streamline event management. Users can explore, register for, and participate in various events organized by different associations and clubs.

Why choose us?

The primary purpose of Event Hub is to facilitate communication and collaboration among different associations and clubs by providing a user-friendly platform to promote and manage their events. It aims to foster community spirit, encourage participation in diverse activities, and enhance the visibility of participating organizations.

How we works

As a visitor on the Event Hub platform, finding links to associations or clubs is easy. Just go to the "Associations" or "Clubs" section on the homepage. You'll see a list of organizations you can check out. Click on any organization's name, and you'll be taken straight to their webpage or profile. It's a simple way to discover and connect with different groups in the community.

Have questions? We have answers!

Passion is the guiding principle for these young entrepreneurs in the local artistic scene, and despite ups and downs, the JAT youth have the ambition to expand this initiative across the regions of Tunisia and to increasingly publicize their activities. An artistic revolution is underway, and these resourceful youth, despite lacking means, are determined to delve into all artistic domains... After all, "art at your fingertips" is not just an empty slogan.

Our association aims to promote and celebrate arts and culture within our local community. We seek to enrich the cultural life of our region by providing opportunities for artistic expression and organizing a variety of cultural events.

Our objective is to create a dynamic and vibrant community where arts and culture are accessible to all. We aim to inspire creativity, foster intercultural dialogue, and contribute to the economic and social development of our region through our artistic and cultural initiatives.

We encourage active participation from our members and the community through workshops, performances, exhibitions, and interactive events. Additionally, we provide volunteer and training opportunities to foster involvement and personal development.

Our programs include art workshops, theatrical performances, concerts, visual art exhibitions, film screenings, cultural festivals, and much more. We continuously seek to diversify our activities to meet the needs and interests of our community.

Project Presentation: DoArt: Lella Khedija This project aims to promote the "environmental heritage" of the village of Hammam Bent Djedidi to develop "alternative and sustainable" tourism in the area. To achieve this, the association will work on establishing a campsite "preserving the nature and surroundings of the location" and organizing clean-up or awareness campaigns. The project will revolve around several main activities: 1. Establishment of an environmentally friendly campsite to develop the "ecotourism" sector in the region. 2. This campsite will facilitate the organization of "eco camps," aimed at bringing people together in nature to exchange ideas and develop projects that offer solutions to environmental issues.
